Home Fish Guide

🐟 Bream

Best Areas:
Rock walls, bridges, creek mouths and structure.

Best Lures:
Small soft plastics, prawns and hard bodies.

Best Colours:
Natural, motor oil, bloodworm.

🐟 Flathead

Best Areas:
Sand flats, weed edges and drop-offs.

Best Lures:
3-5 inch soft plastics.

Best Colours:
Pink, white and natural colours.

🐟 Snapper

Best Areas:
Reefs, rubble and deeper structure.

Best Lures:
Soft plastics, squid and bait.

Best Colours:
Pearl, red and bright colours.
