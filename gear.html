🪶 Bream Setup

Rod:
1-3kg or 2-4kg light spin rod

Reel:
1000-2500 size spinning reel

Line:
6-10lb braid with fluorocarbon leader

Lures:
2-3 inch soft plastics and small hard bodies

🐟 Flathead Setup

Rod:
2-5kg or 3-6kg spin rod

Reel:
2500-3000 size reel

Line:
10-15lb braid with leader

Lures:
3-5 inch soft plastics

🌊 Bigger Fish Setup

Target Fish:
Snapper, Jewfish, GT and other predators

Recommended:
Stronger rods, heavier braid and tougher leaders
